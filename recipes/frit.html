Fritatta Recipe

This is the first recipe I ever learned how to make! It's wicked easy (as long as you have a cast iron skillet) and is exceptional leftover. I usually make it at the start of the work wee kand have a slice for breakfast every day.

Ingredients

  • 12 Eggs
  • Handful of cherry tomatoes
  • feta cheese
  • Fresh herbs of your choosing
  • 1 Potato
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 can Northern or Black Beans

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in the skillet
  2. Slice the potato into medallions about 1/4" thick
  3. Arrange the potatoes on the bottom of the skillet to cover the surface
  4. While you're waiting, quarter your tomatoes and drain and rinse your beans
  5. Flip your potato medallions and salt and pepper them
  6. Whisk your eggs in a separate bowl
  7. Add your beans, and half of your herbs to the skillet and sautee lightly
  8. Pour the eggs in the skillet and cover
  9. After the middle of the frittata has started to set, add the tomatoes, herbs and feta cheese to the top
  10. Sprinkle the feta on top, and put your oven on the 'broil' setting
  11. Move the cast iron into the oven under the broiler. You want the top of the frittata to cook, and veggies to wilt a ltitle.
  12. Remove, and let sit before dividing it up between your friends :)
